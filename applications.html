How to update

The sheet is the source of truth. To track an application past the "applied" stage, fill these columns on the Applied tab (add the headers once in row 1 if missing): Status · Exam Date · Exam Centre · Admit Card (link or date) · Result · Notes. Recognised Status values: applied, admit card out, exam scheduled, exam done, interview, qualified, selected, not qualified, withdrawn — anything else shows as-is. Dates can be written like 12 Apr or 27 July 2026. Registration numbers are shown masked; the Password column is never read — move passwords out of the sheet.

Reads the public Google Sheet live on every page load (no caching). If the page shows a load error, check the sheet is still shared as "anyone with the link can view".
